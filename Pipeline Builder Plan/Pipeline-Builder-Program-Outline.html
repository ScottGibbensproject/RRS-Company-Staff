Pipeline Builder Program

RRS Company - Software Reseller Lead Generation Resource

Program Overview

The Pipeline Builder Program is a subscription resource for RRS software resellers to grow their portfolio through prospecting and lead generation support.

Qualification Requirements:
  • Monthly Subscription: $29/month
  • Must complete RRS Advanced Training

Phase 1: Initial Setup (Before Launch)

1.1 Company Workflow Development

  • Document complete onboarding workflow
  • Create step-by-step process maps
  • Define roles and responsibilities
  • Establish turnaround times and SLAs

1.2 GHL Training Module Setup

Create training module in GoHighLevel covering:

Topic Description
What We Do Services included in Pipeline Builder Program
What We Don't Do Clear boundaries and exclusions
What to Expect Timeline, deliverables, communication cadence
How to Use GHL GoHighLevel platform training and best practices
Expectations Reseller responsibilities and participation requirements

1.3 GHL Intake Form Setup

  • Build intake form in GoHighLevel
  • Configure form fields
  • Set up automation triggers on form submission
  • Create notification workflow for RRS team

1.4 Payment & Subscription Setup

  • Create $29/month subscription product in GHL
  • Set up payment button/checkout page
  • Configure recurring billing
  • Create confirmation emails/automations
  • Link subscription to training module access

Phase 2: Implementation (Reseller Workflow)

1

Sign-Up & Payment

Reseller subscribes ($29/month via GHL), receives automatic access to training module, and welcome email is triggered.

2

Training Completion

Reseller completes Pipeline Builder training in GHL covering program details and platform usage.

3

Intake Form Submission

Reseller completes intake form in GHL specifying target industries, geographic focus, company size preferences, and lead volume goals.

4

List Building & Approval

RRS creates prospect list based on intake criteria, sends for reseller approval before campaign launch.

5

Campaign Execution

RRS manages postcard/mailing campaign and email campaign via Mailchimp.

6

Results & Follow-Up

Leads delivered to reseller who follows up and closes deals.

Intake Form Fields

Field Purpose
Target Industry/Vertical Define prospect business types
Geographic Focus City, state, or region to target
Exclusions/Restrictions Industries or companies to avoid
Current Pipeline Status Existing leads or starting fresh
Monthly Lead Volume Goals Expected volume of leads desired

Campaign Deliverables

Postcard & Mailing Campaign

  • Finalize mailing list
  • Customize messaging
  • Submit to print/mail vendor
  • Track delivery and response
  • Report results

Email Campaign (Mailchimp)

  • Import prospect list
  • Segment audience
  • Schedule email sequence
  • Monitor engagement
  • Provide performance reports
