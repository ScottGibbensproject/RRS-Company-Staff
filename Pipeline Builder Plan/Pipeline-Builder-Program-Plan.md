# Pipeline Builder Program
## RRS Company - Software Reseller Lead Generation Resource

---

## Program Overview

The Pipeline Builder Program is a subscription resource for RRS software resellers to grow their portfolio through prospecting and lead generation support.

### Qualification Requirements
- **Monthly Subscription**: $29/month
- **Training Requirement**: Must complete RRS Advanced Training

## Phase 2: Implementation (Reseller Sign-Up Workflow)

### 2.1 Reseller Intake Process

**Step 1: Sign-Up & Payment**
- Reseller completes payment ($29/month) via GHL
- Automatic access granted to GHL training module
- Welcome email triggered with next steps

**Step 2: Training Completion**
- Reseller completes Pipeline Builder training in GHL
- Training covers program details and GHL usage
- Completion unlocks intake form access

**Step 3: Intake Form Submission (GHL)**
Reseller completes intake form in GHL to clarify prospecting areas:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Target Industry/Vertical | Define prospect business types |
| Geographic Focus | City, state, or region to target |
| Exclusions/Restrictions | Any industries or companies to avoid |
| Current Pipeline Status | Existing leads or starting fresh |
| Monthly Lead Volume Goals | Expected volume of leads desired |

### 2.2 List Building Procedure

**Step 1: List Creation**
- Review intake form responses from GHL
- Source prospect data based on criteria
- Clean and verify list data
- Format list for campaign use

**Step 2: List Approval**
- Send list preview to reseller for review
- Obtain approval before campaign launch
- Make adjustments if needed

### 2.3 Postcard & Mailing Campaign

**Campaign Workflow:**
1. Finalize mailing list from approved prospects
2. Customize postcard messaging (if applicable)
3. Submit to print/mail vendor
4. Track delivery and response rates
5. Report results to reseller

### 2.4 Email Campaign (Mailchimp)

**Campaign Workflow:**
1. Import approved prospect list to Mailchimp
2. Segment audience based on intake criteria
3. Schedule email sequence
4. Monitor deliverability and engagement
5. Provide performance reports to reseller

## Workflow Summary

```
RESELLER JOURNEY
================

1. QUALIFY
   └── Complete Advanced Training
   └── Subscribe ($29/month via GHL)

2. ONBOARD
   └── Complete Pipeline Builder Training (GHL)
   └── Submit Intake Form (GHL)

3. EXECUTE
   └── List Building (RRS creates prospect list)
   └── Postcard/Mail Campaign (RRS manages)
   └── Email Campaign via Mailchimp (RRS manages)

4. RESULTS
   └── Leads delivered to reseller
   └── Reseller follows up and closes deals
```
