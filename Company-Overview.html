Company Overview

Who we are and what we do
Legal Entity
Rate Remover Software, Inc.
Delaware C-Corporation (formed August 2025)
Company Type
SaaS + Payments
Dual pricing software & merchant processing
Target Market
Auto Repair Shops
Independent single & small multi-location shops
Active Merchants
17
Auto repair shops on the platform

Core Value Proposition

  • Enables compliant dual pricing (cash vs card pricing) — not surcharging
  • Automatically calculates card prices by embedding processing costs into pricing
  • Legal in all 50 states, aligned with card brand rules and state regulations
  • Tightly integrated with payment processing workflows
  • Built specifically for the auto repair industry

Strategic Position

  • Standalone SaaS product that also serves as a strategic acquisition engine for merchant services residual revenue
  • Partners with Electronic Payments Inc. (EPI) for merchant account processing
  • Payment gateway via Dejavoo/iPOSpays
  • Reseller program (Pipeline Builder) to scale merchant acquisition
Strategic Guardrails Training is mandatory for all closing resellers. Quality of merchants > quantity. Software quality and compliance take priority over short-term growth. Does not support agnostic processing at scale.
